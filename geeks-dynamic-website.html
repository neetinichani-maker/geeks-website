About Geeks for America

A movement for science-driven progress

Our Mission

Geeks for America is a grassroots movement of scientists, engineers, technologists, and informed citizens dedicated to ensuring that science, evidence, and expertise inform public policy.

We believe that progress comes through innovation, that facts matter, and that America's future depends on our willingness to invest in research, education, and technology.

We work to elect candidates who understand science, who will defend public institutions, and who recognize that solving America's biggest challenges requires evidence-based solutions.

Our Core Values

🔬 Evidence-Based

We trust in the scientific method and peer-reviewed research as the foundation for understanding our world.

🚀 Progress

America's strength comes from innovation and our willingness to solve hard problems through science.

🇺🇸 Patriotic

We believe science is patriotic. It's not partisan—it strengthens our nation and secures our future.
